Frequently Asked Questions

What is Minecraft Jar?

Welcome to Minecraft Jar (also known as MCJar), where you can access and download a variety of MINECRAFT SERVER JARs.


Minecraft Jar only provides you direct links to where you can install all the possible minecraft jars. Minecraft Jar is not affiliated with Mojang or Minecraft. We are just a third party website that provides you with the latest minecraft server jars for Vanilla Minecraft, SpigotMC, etcetera.

Minecraft Jar is free of charge with no additional costs. We do not charge for any of our services.

What devices are supported?

Minecraft Jars does not collect any personal information from its users. We do not require any personal information from you, such as your email address, name, or any other personal information. As a result, we do not have a Privacy Policy or Terms of Service.
